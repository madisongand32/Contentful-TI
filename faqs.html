FAQs

How do I get certified?

  1. Complete the free learning path to prepare.
    Highly recommended but optional for customers; required for partners.
    Learning path: Contentful Professional Certification preparation
    PARTNERS complete this learning path.
    Learning path: Contentful Content Manager Certification preparation
    PARTNERS complete this learning path.
  2. Go to the exam page and enroll, then take the exam.
    Cost: $250 per exam, bundle purchasing available | Payable by credit card or coupon code
    Exam: Contentful Certified Professional Exam
    PARTNERS take this exam.
    Exam: Contentful Certified Content Manager Exam
    PARTNERS take this exam.
  3. After passing, you’re Contentful Certified! Don’t forget to accept your badge from the Credly email you receive.
    If you don’t already have a Credly account, you will be prompted to create a free Credly account.
  4. Share your Credly badge with your professional network on LinkedIn or any other social media platform!

My certification is about to expire. How can I extend my certification?

When your certification is about to expire, you’ll get an email with re-certification instructions. After it expires, you’ll get another email with a link to the re-certification exam. If you do not find this email, please submit a Learning Services support ticket and we will enroll you.

What are the Contentful Certified Professional and Contentful Certified Content Manager badges?

These badges are issued through Credly, making it easy to share and verify your achievements online. They show employers and peers what you did to earn your credential and what skills you’ve gained. You can share your badge directly from Credly to LinkedIn, Facebook, by email, on a website, or in your email signature.

How do I access my Contentful Certified Professional or Contentful Certified Content Manager badge?

Upon passing the Contentful Certified Professional exam, you will receive an email from Credly with instructions on how to claim and share your badge.

What if I already have a Credly account or change my email address?

If you already have a Credly account, simply log in to access your badge—no extra steps needed.
If you change your organization or email address, follow these steps to keep your badge linked to your account:
  1. Log in to Credly.
  2. Go to Settings from your profile menu.
  3. Select “Merge an account” to combine accounts if needed.
  4. You can also add a personal email under Settings to keep access regardless of your work email.
